Students house

A student house is a vibrant space bustling with youthful energy and camaraderie.

  • Common Area
    A cozy space with a mix of thrifted furniture, a well-loved sofa, and a coffee table strewn with study materials.
  • Kitchen
    A hub of activity where pots and pans clink, adorned with a whiteboard on the fridge for reminders and chore assignments.
  • Personalized Bedrooms
    Each room reflects the inhabitant's personality, featuring posters, personal belongings, and sometimes organized chaos.
  • Backyard
    A serene retreat with a hammock or seating area under the shade, providing a peaceful escape from studies.
